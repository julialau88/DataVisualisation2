A CLOSER LOOK AT DIABETES

A visualisation by Julia Lau

Diabetes Prevalence

Diabetes prevalence refers to the percentage of population that has diabetes. Malaysia is one of the countries that has seen a great rise in diabetes prevalence since 2010. Scroll the map to zoom in and out, drag to move the map and hover over each country to view more details.

Diabetic Malaysians

Nearly 1 in 5 Malaysians have diabetes. Malaysia has the highest rate of diabetes in Asia. There is an estimated of 3.9 million Malaysians aged 18 and above have diabetes.

Male or Female?

Men are more likely to be diagnosed with diabetes. In Malaysia, more than half of diabetes patients are males. One of the reasons is that men store more fat in their bellies.

Killers of Malaysia

Diabetes is one of the leading causes of deaths in Malaysia due to its high prevalenece. It is within the top 5 causes of deaths in Malaysia.

Diabetes On the Rise

It is predicted that there will be a 2% rise in cases of diabetes in Malaysia from 2010 to 2045. In the future, there will be more population with diabetes if steps are not taken to curb this..
